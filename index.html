Why Us?

Job recruitment is a very tedious task, it involves multiple interviews, requires several resources, and it's time-consuming. It is also a tiring process for a candidate. Candidates need to look out for multiple opportunities suited for their profile and skillset.

Missing Hiring Targets

Many teams miss hiring targets by more than 55% due to lack of standard interview process and the expertise of interviewers.

Less than 25% of the team have the expertise to do so with the consistency needed to get predictable results.

To help recruiters and job seekers, we have designed a sophisticated and elegant system at ProHyred.

Solutions

RECRUITERS

When Recruiters only have the Job Requirement:

Recruiters can send us the job requirement. We find the candidates and do all the screenings based on JD and skills required.

We share the PAT results along with recommendation and feedback so that Recruiters can take the final decision on bringing qualified candidates onsite.

When Recruiters already have your candidates for Interview :

Recruiters can send the list of candidates for the PAT Interview process, and we do all the screenings based on JD and skills required.

We share the PAT results along with recommendation and feedback so that Recruiters can take the final decision on bringing qualified candidates onsite..

Job Seekers

Job Seekers can register on our platform and take up our ProHyred Aptitude Test (PAT) at no cost.

Based on their score and skillsets, they will then be bucketed into various categories of Qualified Candidates Pool.

They don't have to go for an initial screening process again and again when they apply to different employers via ProHyred.

We share their interview feedback with employers based on which the Recruiters will call them onsite for the final hiring process.
