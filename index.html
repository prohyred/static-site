Why Us?

It is a time and resource-consuming process for an organization, where they have to go through multiple profiles to shortlist a candidate.

We are here to streamline the process where organizations needn't go through multiple profiles instead, they can ask candidates to register here and get screened before face to face interviews with the organization.

Missing Hiring Targets

Many teams miss hiring targets by more than 55% due to lack of standard interview process and the expertise of interviewers.

Less than 25% of the team have the expertise to do so with the consistency needed to get predictable results.

To help recruiters and job seekers, we have designed a sophisticated and elegant system at ProHyred.

Solutions

RECRUITERS

When you only have the Job Requirement:

You can send us the job requirement. We find the candidates and do all the screenings based on JD and skills required.

We share the PAT results along with recommendation and feedback so that you can take the final decision on bringing qualified candidates onsite.

When you already have your candidates for Interview :

You can send the list of candidates for the PAT Interview process, and we do all the screenings based on JD and skills required.

We share the PAT results along with recommendation and feedback so that you can take the final decision on bringing qualified candidates onsite..

Job Seekers

You can register on our platform and take up our ProHyred Aptitude Test (PAT) at no cost.

Based on your score and skillsets, they will then be bucketed into various categories of Qualified Candidates Pool.

You don't have to go for an initial screening process again and again when you apply to different employers via ProHyred.

We share your interview feedback with employers based on which the Recruiters will call you onsite for the final hiring process.
